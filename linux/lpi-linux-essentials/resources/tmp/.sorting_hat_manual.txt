[DEPLOYMENT NOTES]
The Sorting Hat script reads student mind-waves and outputs house assignments.
To initiate a manual diagnostic scan across the engine array, execute:
    # /usr/local/bin/sorting_hat --analyze-mind

[KNOWN BUGS]
If a student exhibits high levels of both ambition and courage, the choice logic 
loops indefinitely. A temporary patch has been applied to wait for an explicit 
override signal from the Headmaster via standard input.

[CONFIGURATION STRATIFICATION]
System paths must be structured exactly as follows for house records:
    /hogwarts/houses/gryffindor
    /hogwarts/houses/slytherin
    /hogwarts/houses/ravenclaw
    /hogwarts/houses/hufflepuff
